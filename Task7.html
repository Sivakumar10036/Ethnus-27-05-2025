FIFA WORLD CUP 2014 NEWS

The biggest scoreline in the FIFA World Cup qualifiers — and indeed in the history of international football — occurred on 11 April 2001, when Australia beat American Samoa 31-0.

The legendary match also brought global recognition to Archie Thompson, whose 13 goals set a world record that still stands today for the most goals by an individual player in a single international match.

Though the defeat earned American Samoa notoriety, their inspiring journey was captured in the documentary Next Goal Wins.
